Who We Are

ACM StudentChapter, Department of Computer Engineering, SVPCET was started in the year 2019, a student society that provides opportunities for students to network, learn, and share knowledge.The chapter conducts seminars, lectures, and workshops for shared networking opportunities with peers and experts across the computing spectrum. Also, these included a series of technical events that offers students both interesting technical content and career opportunities.

Our Values

Core values lead the way, let's keep them in play!

Mission

SVPCET, ACM Student Chapter is a educational club dedicated to organizing the various events based on various field of Computer Science for enhancing the students knowledge.

Vision

SVPCET, ACM Student Chapter will be continuosly serving the students with great knowledge and latest technologies in field of Computer Science.

Technical Excellence. Education and technical Advancement. Ethical computing and technology for positive impact.
